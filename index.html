Meet Your Teacher

Remya Bhaskaran

Hi, I'm Remya — a teacher with over 20 years of experience helping students understand concepts deeply, not just memorise them. I started RRT Academy to create a calm, supportive space for students who genuinely want to learn and grow.

I am a B.Tech graduate in Electronics & Communication Engineering from CUSAT. My teaching journey began in 2005 — right after completing my Plus Two — and has been a continuous, passionate journey ever since.

From 2020 to 2024, I served as a Workshop Instructor in Electronics at Government Technical School, Kaliyar — bringing structured, practical teaching into everything I do. Through years of working with students who feared Maths, I developed a method built on real understanding, not memorisation.
